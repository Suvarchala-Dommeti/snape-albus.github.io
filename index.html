IIT ROORKEE

Stats & Ranking: NIRF 2019: Ranked 6th Established in 1847 during British Rule 365 Acres Campus
Connectivity: By Car: Traveling from Delhi, Roorkee is located towards north approximately 170 kilometres By Air: 70 Km from Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun By Railways: By train, it takes less than 3 hours and 30 minutes to reach Roorkee from Delhi, so it is the fastest and most economical way of travel.
Fees: Per semester: Institute Fees + Hostel Fees+ Mess Fees : Rs. 1,46,250
Mode of Admission: JEE-Main followed by JEE-Advanced Apply through JoSAA Counselling
Streams offered and Seat Matrix Branch Seats
Sports Facilities:
  • Olympic sized swimming pool
  • 3 basketball courts with floodlights & electronic scoreboard
  • Indoor badminton hall, apart from badminton courts present in hostels
  • Rowing facility (Ganges river)
  • Cricket field with A.C. pavilion, bowling machine & 3 training pitches
  • International standard football ground
  • Hockey stadium with stands for spectators
  • Grass as well as Synthetic courts for Lawn Tennis
  • 4 indoor squash courts
  • Table Tennis room, hostels have T.T. tables
  • Volleyball ground
Placements: This year the overall average CTC turned out to be 14.727 Lakh INR. Microsoft Redmond and Uber International offered the highest paying packages of INR 1.5 crores and INR 1.20 Crores respectively. Uber India, Microsoft, JP Morgan, Mercari were among the other high payers with packages above 30 Lakh INR. Companies that came to hire for software profiles offered an average of INR 23.15 Lakhs whereas core engineering profiles offered an average CTC of INR 10.19 Lakhs. Read more on: http://watchout.iitr.ac.in/2019/06/placements-at-iitr
Faculty: Faculty is good, some of the professors have excellent teaching methods, while you’ll find some who will bore you with their PPTs.
Hostel:
  • Hostels nhi, Bhawans!
  • 10 boys’ hotel, 3 girls’ hostel
  • Freshmen get Rajendra Bhawan, which is arguably the best hostel at IITR
  • Rooms for freshmen are either single or double
  • Rooms are allotted based on Jee AIR, most of the top rankers get single rooms, i.e mostly CSE and ECE students.
  • Toilets are cleaned everyday but there are some loopholes in sanitation
  • Bed, Almirah, Fan, Study Table, Plug Points, LAN cable are there in every room
  • Internet is top notch
  • Every Bhawan has its own gym
  • Every Bhawan has 1 canteen too
  • TV room, barber shop, stationary shop, washing machine, etc. are there too
Hostel Mess:
  • Mess food is not like food but like a medicine that gives energy for daily work
  • No variations in meals as well, food is bland
  • Food is good in the starting, but as time passes, quality decreases exponentially
  • But the Bhawan canteens will keep you satisfied, for your midnight cravings
Notable Alumni:
  • Mr. Jaiprakash Gaur: Founder, Jaypee Group of Companies
  • Mr. Mangu Singh: Chairman, DMRC
  • Mr. Pradip Baijal: IAS and former Chairman, TRAI
  • Mr. Amit Singal: Former Senior Vice President, Google
  • Mr. Naveen Jain: Founder, InfoSpace
  • Mr. Vineet Khatri: Prominent Educator, Unacademy
Miscellaneous:
  • Thomso is the annual cultural fest of IIT Roorkee
  • Jubin Nautiyal, Salim-Sulaiman, Farhan Akhtar have performed
  • During winters, the temperature falls down to as low as 1–20 oC. In summers the temperature goes up to as high as 41–43 oC.
